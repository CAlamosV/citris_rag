Name: David Lee

Description: David T. Lee is an Assistant Professor of Technology Management at UC Santa Cruz. He received his PhD from Stanford University, where he was a part of the Social Algorithms Lab and Crowdsourced Democracy Team, and his BS from the California Institute of Technology. His research centers around using HCI and algorithms to make it possible for governments and NGOs to mobilize and coordinate citizens at scale towards addressing complex, societal issues. His work has received a Notable Paper Award at HCOMP, been published in the Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences (PNAS), and deployed with the Finland Ministry of the Environment.
