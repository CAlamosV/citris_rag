Name: Yueyue Fan

Description: Yueyue Fan is a professor at the UC Davis department of Civil and Environmental Engineering. Her research interests include transportation and renewable energy infrastructure system modeling and optimization, critical transportation and energy infrastructure protection, adaptive network routing and resource allocation processes, and stochastic and dynamic system modeling and computational methods.
